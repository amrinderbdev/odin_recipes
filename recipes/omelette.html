Classic Omelette

Description

The classic omelette is a masterpiece of simplicity, transforming humble eggs into a sophisticated, protein-packed meal in minutes.

Ingredients Requirements:-

  • 2 or 3 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
  • A pinch of kosher salt
  • A pinch of freshly ground black pepper (white pepper is traditional for a clean look)
  • 1 tablespoon fresh chives, finely chopped (optional garnish)
  • 2 tablespoons shredded cheese, such as Gruy√®re or Cheddar (optional filling)

Steps

  1. In a small bowl, crack the eggs and whisk them vigorously with a fork until no streaks of white remain. Season with a pinch of salt and pepper.
  2. Place a non-stick skillet over medium-low heat and add the butter. Let it melt until it starts to foam but doesn't brown.
  3. Pour the egg mixture into the skillet. Using a heat-resistant spatula, gently move the eggs around to create small curds.
  4. Once the eggs are mostly set but still slightly runny on top, use the spatula to smoothen them into an even layer.
  5. If using cheese, sprinkle it across the center of the eggs now.
  6. Carefully fold one-third of the omelette over the center, then slide it onto a plate, folding it again so it lands seam-side down.
  7. Garnish with fresh chives and serve immediately while hot.
