The Perfect Classic Pizza

Description

This classic homemade pizza features a soft yet crispy crust, a rich tomato sauce, and a gooey layer of perfectly melted mozzarella cheese.

Ingredients Requirements:-

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 ¼ teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • ½ cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 450°F (230°C).
  2. In a small bowl, combine warm water, sugar, and yeast; let it sit until frothy.
  3. Mix the flour, salt, and olive oil into the yeast mixture until a dough forms.
  4. Knead the dough on a floured surface for 5 minutes, then let it rest in a covered bowl for 30 minutes.
  5. Roll the dough into a circle and place it on a greased pizza pan or baking sheet.
  6. Spread a thin layer of pizza sauce over the dough, leaving room for the crust.
  7. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese and any desired toppings evenly over the sauce.
  8. Bake for 10-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbling.
  9. Remove from the oven, let it cool for a minute, slice, and enjoy!
