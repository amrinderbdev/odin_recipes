French Fries

Description

There is nothing quite like a batch of perfectly golden, double-fried French fries.

These fries feature a remarkably crunchy exterior that shatters upon impact, revealing a fluffy, cloud-like potato interior that is seasoned to perfection with just a touch of sea salt.

Ingredients Requirements:-

  • 4 large Russet potatoes (best for starch content)
  • 2 quarts vegetable oil (for frying)
  • Sea salt to taste
  • Optional: Freshly cracked black pepper or paprika
  • Optional: 1 tablespoon white vinegar (for the soaking water)

Steps

  1. Peel the potatoes (optional) and cut them into even sticks about 1/4 to 1/2 inch thick.
  2. Place the cut potatoes in a large bowl of cold water and let them soak for at least 30 minutes to remove excess starch.
  3. Drain the potatoes and pat them completely dry with a clean kitchen towel or paper towels.

  5. Working in batches, fry the potatoes for about 3-5 minutes until they are soft but not yet browned. Remove them and let them drain on paper towels.
  6. Increase the oil temperature to 375째F (190째C).
  7. Fry the potatoes a second time for 2-3 minutes until they are golden brown and perfectly crispy.
  8. Remove from the oil, immediately toss with sea salt in a large bowl, and serve hot!
